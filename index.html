Hi, my name is Connor and I am an undergraduate student at Carnegie Mellon University studying computer science and materials science. I am particularly interested in algorithms, the theory of computation, and their connections toward practical application. Recently, I have become especially interested in quantum computation and information because it unifies so many compelling ideas from computer science, physics, and math.

I aspire to apply my skills and knowledge toward developing the theory behind technologies with the potential to make near-term, real-world impacts. My work relating to solar cells has been just the beginning of this, and I hope to use my experience in simulation to advance quantum information science.
